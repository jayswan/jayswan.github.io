--- layout: post title: Summarize Router Throughput in Packets/Second date: '2011-11-10T09:52:00.001-07:00' author: Jay Swan tags: modified_time: '2011-11-10T09:52:30.040-07:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-5029689981158113588.post-1890661937424239367 blogger_orig_url: http://unroutable.blogspot.com/2011/11/summarize-router-throughput-in.html --- Here's a quick one-liner to summarize the current packets-per-second throughput on an entire IOS router:

$ ssh 10.1.2.3 'sh int summ' | awk '/^*/{RXPPS+=$8;TXPPS+=$10} END {print "RXPPS=" RXPPS,"TXPPS=" TXPPS}'

RXPPS=1613 TXPPS=1539
