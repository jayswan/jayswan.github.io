--- layout: post title: simple exclusion filters date: '2009-12-08T17:35:00.002-07:00' author: Jay Swan tags: modified_time: '2009-12-08T17:37:27.852-07:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-5029689981158113588.post-5667920424984028273 blogger_orig_url: http://unroutable.blogspot.com/2009/12/simple-exclusion-filters.html --- I use these constantly (and many others, but these come first to mind):

display only interfaces with assigned IP addresses:
sh ip int b | e una

display only active switch interfaces:
sh int status | e not

display CDP neighbors, except phones:
sh cdp n | e SEP
